EchoGPT Privacy Policy

When you use EchoGPT, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you use EchoGPT.

What data do we collect?

We collect data that you provide to us when you use EchoGPT, such as your Discord username and any messages or commands that you send to EchoGPT We also collect usage data, such as how often you use EchoGPT and which commands you use.

How do we use your data?

We use your data to provide and improve EchoGPT, to respond to your requests and support needs, and to personalize your experience. We may also use your data to communicate with you about our products and services.

How do we share your data?

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your data with service providers who help us to operate EchoGPT or who provide other services to us. We may also share your data with law enforcement or other authorities if required by law.

How do we protect your data?

We use reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

How can you control your data?

You can request that we delete your personal data at any time by contacting us at echo-the-coder@tuta.io.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
